Signing Up

Click the nextcloud instance link. Once you get there you can click register and enter your email. You should then get a verification email from cloudtetricz@gmail.com. This will contain a link to confirm your email and finish your resgistraion.

Note: You can also login with either a Google Account or with a Github Account.

After you finish this I will need to approve your account, so send me a text and I will get it setup :)

Recommendations

  • Use 2 factor authentication for your account
  • Backing up any photos you have before uploading to my nextcloud instance using the automated features.

Setup for PC

Download your respective client and install.

Open the app and click login to nextcloud. The server address is ; https://nextcloud.tetricz.com/ ; Once entered the application will open using a web browser to authenticate the program. Login and grant permissions.

Setup for iPhone

Download the app from the appstore. Open https://nextcloud.tetricz.com/ on a browser on a computer. Go to settings > security > create new app password. It should be at the bottom.

Give it a name and create new app password, you should see a "SHOW QR CODE FOR MOBILE DEVICES" below. Once you have the QR code open up the nextcloud app and tap the QR code logo to open the scanner. Scan the QR code and continue to setup auto upload and more features.
